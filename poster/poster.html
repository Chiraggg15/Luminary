Introduction

In the modern job market, 75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before ever reaching a human recruiter. Manual resume tailoring is time-consuming and prone to overlooking critical keywords.

The AI-Powered Resume Generator solves this gap by empowering job seekers with intelligent automation that ensures their professional profile is both reader-friendly and machine-optimized.

Project Aim

To design and develop a comprehensive digital ecosystem that prioritizes ATS-compliance while integrating high-fidelity AI content generation. The goal is to provide a seamless transition from job discovery to application-ready documents.

Key Objectives

  • AI Content Engine: Implement Gemini 1.5 Flash to generate context-aware summaries and quantifiable experience bullets.
  • NLP Analytics: Develop a custom matching engine (spaCy) to calculate real-time similarity scores vs Job Descriptions.
  • Career Suite: Integrate Cover Letter generation and AI-driven mock interview preparation.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Build a robust Flask/MongoDB backend with Google SSO for seamless access.
